Low-Cost Acoustic Monitoring for CNC Milling

Active research TU Dortmund · Lamarr 2025–Present

Evaluating whether low-cost microphones (B5, HI10, TASCAM) can capture chatter-relevant acoustic information compared with a PCB reference microphone — toward physics-informed, cost-effective industrial sensing.

Approach & details

Approach: Comparing PCB, B5, HI10, and TASCAM recordings using spectrogram similarity, dominant-frequency behavior, tooth-pass harmonics, and impact-hammer-based resonance interpretation.

Impact: Contributes toward physics-informed, cost-effective acoustic sensing for industrial machining monitoring.
