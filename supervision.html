Supervision Philosophy

I believe in guiding students to develop independent research thinking rather than providing ready answers. Each student brings unique strengths — my role is to help them identify their research questions clearly, build rigorous methodology, and communicate their work with precision.

For students interested in Industrial AI, machining stability, acoustic monitoring, or explainable ML, I am particularly well-positioned to offer deep technical guidance aligned with active research projects at TU Dortmund and the Lamarr Institute.
