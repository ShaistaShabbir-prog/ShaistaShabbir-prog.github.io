Supervision Philosophy

I guide students toward independent research thinking rather than providing ready answers. Each student has unique strengths — my role is to help them identify their research question clearly, build a rigorous methodology, and communicate findings with precision.

For students interested in Industrial AI, machining stability, acoustic monitoring, explainable ML, or applied NLP, I am well-positioned to offer deep technical guidance aligned with active research at TU Dortmund and the Lamarr Institute.
