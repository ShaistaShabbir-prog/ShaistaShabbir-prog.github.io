Current Research Manuscripts · TU Dortmund / Lamarr Institute

AudioIno: Expert-Guided Annotation for Machining Stability and Chatter Analysis

Work in progress Shaista Shabbir · TU Dortmund University / Lamarr Institute 2026

Presents the AudioIno expert-guided labeling and analysis platform for machining stability annotation, covering stable, metastable, and chatter regions in acoustic and force-assisted machining experiments. Discusses annotation methodology, spectrogram-based workflows, and dataset design for Industrial AI.

Machining Stability Chatter Detection Human-in-the-loop Industrial AI

Low-Cost Acoustic Monitoring for CNC Milling: Spectral Fidelity and Resonance-Guided Interpretation

Work in progress Shaista Shabbir · TU Dortmund University / Lamarr Institute 2026

Investigates the spectral fidelity of low-cost microphones (B5, HI10, TASCAM) against a PCB reference microphone for capturing chatter-relevant acoustic information in CNC milling. Analysis includes spectrogram similarity, dominant-frequency behavior, tooth-pass harmonics, and resonance interpretation via impact hammer testing.

Acoustic Monitoring Low-Cost Sensing Machining Dynamics Signal Processing

Tool Wear Prediction from Multimodal Machining Signals

Work in progress Student thesis collaboration · TU Dortmund University 2026

Investigates tool condition monitoring using force, temperature, and acoustic machining signals. Addresses shortcut learning, generalization across cutting conditions, and ablation analysis of feature contributions to CNN and traditional ML models.
